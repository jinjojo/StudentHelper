The ELITE Leadership Academy is a leadership development program for high school students that takes them to the

High School Diplomats

The High School Diplomats Program is a cultural enrichment experience that brings together high school students from Japan and the United States.

HSD is a ten-day program at Princeton University for American students to interact and forge friendships with Japanese students. During the stay each

American student is paired with a Japanese roommate. The program is designed to expose the students to both cultures through daily themes and activities.

http://www.highschooldiplomats.com
