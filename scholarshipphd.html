Ph. D

Alice Lawson Scholarship

This scholarship is for women from Chester County, Pennsylvania. To qualify for this scholarship, applicants must be completing post-secondary education and have already successfully completed at least 60 credits of study. Award amount varies. To apply, contact Sue Johnston of the American Association of University Women.

The Cory L. Richards Memorial Scholarship Program, Sponsored by the Guttmacher Institute
One individual will be designated each year as a Cory Richards Scholar and awarded a one-time scholarship of $15,000 to support full-time graduate study at an accredited institution in the United States. To apply, click here!
